strace, lsof, ss. The tools that verify what a process actually does, not what it claims to do.

## Key Topics

- strace - tracing system calls to see actual kernel interactions
- lsof - listing open files, sockets, and pipes
- ss - socket statistics and connection state
- Filtering and interpreting strace output
- Diagnosing why an agent-spawned process hangs or fails
- The principle: observe behaviour, do not trust output
- Combining observation tools to build a complete picture
